About

The Natural Capital Project Data Hub provides analysis-ready ecosystem services data as well as data for and from InVEST®, our suite of free, open-source ecosystem service models. With this Data Hub, we intend to make it faster and simpler for anyone to create and use ecosystem services information for a wide range of analyses and decisions. Contact us with feedback about the site or suggestions for open access datasets to add.

The Natural Capital Project, based at Stanford University, is a partnership between interdisciplinary researchers, professionals, and leaders working to make valuing natural capital easier and more accessible to all. To read more about the Natural Capital Project and InVEST, please visit our website.
